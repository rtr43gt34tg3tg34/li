Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Liam's Websites handles your information when you use our site at liamswebsites.store.

Information We Collect

When you submit a booking, we collect your name, email address, business name, business type, budget, and project notes. This is submitted directly via the Gmail API using your Google account.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to respond to your booking and deliver the services you've requested. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Google Sign-In & Gmail API

Our booking form uses Google OAuth and the Gmail API (gmail.send and gmail.readonly scopes) to send booking emails and let you view replies. We do not store your emails or access data beyond what you authorize.

Data Storage

We do not store personal information on our servers. Your access token is stored locally in your browser only. Chat messages are stored in memory and reset when the server restarts.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or analytics tools.

Third-Party Services

We use Google OAuth and the Gmail API. Your use is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personal information by contacting us at LiamsSites@proton.me.

Contact

Questions? Email LiamsSites@proton.me.
