Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Liam's Websites ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our website at liamswebsites.store.

Information We Collect

When you submit a booking request through our site, we collect the following information you provide: your name, email address, business name, business type, budget, and project notes. This information is submitted directly to our email via the Gmail API using your Google account.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your booking request and deliver the website services you've requested. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Google Sign-In & Gmail API

Our booking form uses Google OAuth to authenticate you and send a booking email on your behalf using the Gmail API. We only request the gmail.send scope, which allows us to send one email per booking submission. We do not read, store, or access any of your existing emails or Google account data beyond what is needed to send the booking email.

Data Storage

We do not store your personal information on our servers. Booking details are delivered directly to our business email inbox and are not retained in any database.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or analytics tools.

Third-Party Services

We use Google OAuth and the Gmail API (provided by Google LLC) to facilitate booking submissions. Your use of Google Sign-In is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personal information you've submitted by contacting us at LiamsSites@proton.me.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at LiamsSites@proton.me.
